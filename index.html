About Kavitha Lab Glass Works

Excellence in Glass Manufacturing Since 1967

55+ Years Experience

Leading Manufacturer of Laboratory & Industrial Glassware

Founded in 1967 by Proprietor Govindaraj, Kavitha Lab Glass Works has been at the forefront of manufacturing premium quality laboratory and industrial glassware in Chennai. Our commitment to excellence and precision has made us a trusted name in the scientific community.

We specialize in producing high-quality borosilicate glassware, teflon ware, and industrial glass products that meet international standards. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility ensures that every product delivers exceptional performance and durability.
